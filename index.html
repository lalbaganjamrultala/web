About us

Lalbagan Jamrultala Saraswati Puja Samity is a well-known community organization established in 1950. The club has been consistently organizing Saraswati Puja along with various social initiatives for the welfare of society.

Social Commitment & Service

The club actively conducts blood donation camps, health camps, and social charity programs, contributing positively to community development and social responsibility.

Cultural Activities (Spandan)

The club also runs a cultural unit named Spandan, which organizes drawing competitions, dance competitions, and other cultural events every year to encourage creativity and talent.
